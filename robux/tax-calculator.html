How to Use the Robux Tax Calculator

Marketplace Tax (30%)

When you sell items on the Roblox marketplace, Roblox automatically deducts a 30% tax from your earnings. This means if you sell an item for 1000 Robux, you'll only receive 700 Robux after the tax is applied.

Reverse Calculation

If you want to receive a specific amount of Robux after tax, you need to calculate backwards. To receive 1000 Robux after the 30% tax, you need to list your item for 1,429 Robux (1000 √∑ 0.7 = 1,428.57, rounded up).

Group Payouts

  • Regular Groups: 30% tax on all payouts
  • Premium Groups: Reduced 10% tax rate
  • Developer Groups: Special 0% tax rate for qualified developers

DevEx Eligibility

To be eligible for Developer Exchange (DevEx), you need:

  • Minimum 50,000 earned Robux
  • Valid tax information on file
  • Good standing account
  • 13+ years old (or parental consent)
