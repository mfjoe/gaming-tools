1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

How Cookies Work

When you visit our website:

  • Your web browser downloads cookies and stores them on your device
  • Cookies contain information like your preferences, session data, or unique identifiers
  • When you return to the website, your browser sends the cookies back to the server
  • This allows the website to "remember" information about your visit

Types of Cookies by Duration

Session cookies: These are temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. They help websites remember what you're doing as you navigate between pages.

Persistent cookies: These cookies remain on your device for a set period (or until you delete them). They remember your preferences across multiple visits.

Types of Cookies by Origin

First-party cookies: Set by the website you're visiting (in this case, gaming-tools.co.uk). We have control over these cookies.

Third-party cookies: Set by external services we use (like Google Analytics or Google AdSense). These are controlled by those third parties.

5. How to Control Cookies

You have several options to control or delete cookies:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies: Prevent websites from setting any cookies
  • Block third-party cookies: Allow first-party cookies but block third-party tracking
  • Delete existing cookies: Remove cookies already stored on your device
  • Get notifications: Be alerted when websites try to set cookies

How to manage cookies in different browsers:

Chrome Instructions Firefox Instructions Safari Instructions Edge Instructions

Warning: Blocking all cookies may affect your experience on our website and other sites. Some features may not work properly, and you may need to re-enter information more frequently.

Opt Out of Google Services

Google Analytics:

  • Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • This prevents your data from being used by Google Analytics across all websites

Google Ads (AdSense):

  • Visit Google Ads Settings to opt out of personalized advertising
  • Use the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool
  • Install browser extensions like uBlock Origin or Privacy Badger (note: this may block ads entirely, affecting our revenue)
Manage Google Ad Preferences Opt Out of Google Analytics

Mobile Device Settings

iOS (iPhone/iPad):

  1. Go to Settings → Safari
  2. Toggle "Prevent Cross-Site Tracking" ON
  3. Toggle "Block All Cookies" ON (if desired, but may break websites)
  4. Tap "Advanced" → "Website Data" to view and delete stored data

Android:

  1. Open Chrome browser
  2. Tap the three dots menu → Settings
  3. Tap "Site settings" → "Cookies"
  4. Choose your cookie preferences
  5. Go to "Site settings" → "All sites" to delete cookies

Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal that requests websites not to track your browsing. However:

  • There is no universal standard for how websites should respond to DNT
  • Many websites, including ours, do not currently respond to DNT signals
  • Third-party services (Google Analytics, AdSense) may not honor DNT
  • For effective tracking prevention, use the specific opt-out tools mentioned above

6. Impact of Blocking Cookies

What Still Works

If you block cookies, our gaming calculators will still function. You can:

  • Use all calculators (Robux, V-Bucks, Minecoins, etc.)
  • Read guides and educational content
  • Navigate the website normally

What May Not Work

However, blocking cookies may affect:

  • Preference memory: We won't remember your calculator settings between visits
  • Cookie consent: We'll ask about cookie preferences on every visit
  • Ad personalization: You'll see generic ads instead of relevant ones
  • Analytics: We won't be able to improve the site based on usage data

How Blocking Affects Us

While we respect your right to block cookies, please understand:

  • Revenue impact: Ad revenue funds our free calculators. Blocking ads may make it harder to maintain the service
  • Improvement limitations: Without analytics, we can't understand which calculators need improvement
  • User experience: We can't optimize the site for your device or preferences

8. International Users and Cookies

GDPR (European Union & UK)

If you're in the EU or UK, you have specific rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Consent required: We must obtain your consent before using non-essential cookies
  • Right to withdraw: You can withdraw consent at any time through browser settings
  • Right to access: You can request information about what data cookies have collected (though most is anonymous)
  • Right to erasure: You can delete cookies and request deletion of associated data

CCPA (California, USA)

California residents have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know: What personal information is collected through cookies
  • Right to opt-out: Opt out of the "sale" of personal information (though we don't sell data, third-party cookies may share it)
  • Right to delete: Request deletion of personal information collected through cookies

Other Regions

Regardless of location, you can:

  • Control cookies through browser settings
  • Opt out of Google Analytics and personalized advertising
  • Contact us with privacy concerns
